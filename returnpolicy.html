Return Policy

1. Returns and Exchanges

At SkY Performance, we strive to provide high-quality performance auto parts to our customers. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy to ensure your satisfaction.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return or exchange, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be in its original packaging.
  • The item must be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • The return request must be initiated within 30 days from the date of purchase.
3. Return Process

To initiate a return or exchange, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our Customer Service team at skyperformance@gmail.com to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
  2. Clearly mark the RMA number on the outside of the package.
  3. Ship the item, along with a copy of the original invoice and a brief explanation of the reason for the return, to the address provided by our Customer Service team.
4. Refund or Exchange

Upon receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund or exchange. If approved:

  • Refund: A credit will be applied to your original method of payment within a specified number of days.
  • Exchange: We will promptly dispatch the replacement item.
5. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are non-returnable, including:

  • Customized or modified parts.
  • Clearance or discounted items.
  • Items damaged due to misuse, negligence, or improper installation.
6. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs for returns and exchanges are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to an error on our part or a defective product.

7. Warranty Information

Many of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty. Please refer to the specific product documentation for warranty details. SkY Performance is not responsible for items that fall outside the warranty period.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy, please contact our Customer Service team at skyperformance@gmail.com. or go to the Contact Page

SkY Performance reserves the right to update or modify this return policy at any time without prior notice.
